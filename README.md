# Christoph Bieritz, CFA | Financial Engineer & Quant Builder

👋 **Hello! I'm Christoph.** I am a CFA Charterholder and Financial Engineer with over a decade of professional experience. My career has been built at the intersection of high-level **Big 4 Advisory** and the fast-paced, high-growth environment of **Berlin Fintech Unicorns**.

I define myself as a **Builder**. I don’t just analyze financial data; I architect the systems, databases, and automated workflows that allow financial institutions to scale and manage risk with precision.

### 🎯 Professional Focus

* **📈 Portfolio Optimization & Risk Management**
    Leveraging modern **Machine Learning** frameworks to enhance decision-making and risk assessment. I am interested in building robust models that provide a competitive edge in portfolio steering and capital allocation.

* **⚡ Financial Engineering & Infrastructure**
    Designing and maintaining the technical "pipes" for complex financial products. I specialize in the end-to-end automation of financial workflows, including the management and securitization (**ABS**) of large-scale debt portfolios.

* **🗄️ Database Architecture & Data Strategy**
    Building dedicated financial databases from the ground up. I transform fragmented data landscapes into scalable, cloud-based environments that allow for real-time reporting and deep-dive analytics.

* **🤖 Workflow Automation**
    I have a passion for eliminating manual bottlenecks. I take complex, week-long financial processes and rebuild them into automated, "push-button" solutions that increase accuracy and free up strategic resources.
